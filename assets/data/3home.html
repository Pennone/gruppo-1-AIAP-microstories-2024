The route

This route focuses on how animals have been interpreted by designers and illustrators,exploring three kinds: surreal, pictographic, and realistic. The goal is to provide an overview of the diverse perspectives and styles that have shaped the artistic and graphic representation of animals.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Interpretation over time

In the graph, artifacts created over time belonging to three styles of interpretation are indicated: surreal, pictographic, and realistic. As expressed by the graph, there is a peak of surreal artifacts between the 60s and 80s.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Interpretation distribution

As shown in the pie chart, the pictographic style is the most frequently used, followed by a certain uniformity between surreal and realistic.
