The Surreal itinerary

Physical and behavioral traits attributed to these animals pretty much don’t match at all their real appearance. It’s possible to find butterflies that play the saxophone, blue horses, pigeons with human heads and a lot more. This comes together to create a fantasy world, where human imagination merges with the animal world.

Have fun following the line and exploring all the animals in this itinerary!

The author that mostly represents this category is Andrea Rauch, italian designer and illustrator active since the 70s, known for his illustrations that challenge the standardized model of expressiveness in graphic language, through psychedelic colors and exaggerated features. His surreal, anthropomorphic animals always have something to say to the viewer.
