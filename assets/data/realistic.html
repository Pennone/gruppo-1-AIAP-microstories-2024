The Realistic itinerary

This section is where one can find animal representations with the aim of reflecting their natural and real appearance. With more of less fidelity, depending on the style of representation, the goal of the depiction is clearly realistic, but that doesn’t necessarily strip it of symbolic connotations.

Have fun following the line and exploring all the animals in this itinerary!

The author that mostly represents this category is Guido Crepax, italian cartoonist active around the 60s 70s and 80s. His realistic and extremely dynamic depictions of animals bring the covers of his comics to life by effectively accompanying the main characters. Most of the animals represented are horses, due to the popularity of Cowboy comics in the 60s.
