The Pictographic itinerary

In this category are gathered those animal representations characterized by stylized and synthetic features. This selection highlights the most iconic of one animal’s traits, the ones that remain impressed the most in the human memory and mind and that get majorly enhanced by graphic designers.

Have fun following the line and exploring all the animals in this itinerary!

The author that mostly represents this category is Silvio Coppola, italian architect and graphic designer who in order to experiment with merging architecture, graphic and product design, founded the ED group with Munari and other great designers. He experimented around the 1970s with a variety of materials to create picturesque images of animals. His designs make up more than 90% of all mixed media animals in the archive.
